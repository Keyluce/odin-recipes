Vada Pav

Description

Vada Pav is a popular street food orginated in Mumbai, India. It is a quick, grab and go meal that's cheap, filling, delicious and a fun-food loved by everyone.

For the uninitiated, Mumbai vada pav is simply Indian style veggie burger that's vegan too. The filling has a flattened Batata Vada, which is a spiced potato patty that's batter fried.

Ingredients

For Vada

  • 2 medium onions, sliced
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp black mustard seeds
  • 7-8 curry leaves
  • 1/4th tsp Asafoetida
  • 2 green chillies, chopped
  • 1/4th tsp Turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 3-4 medium potatoes, boiled
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp coriander, chopped
  • Oil for deep frying

For Batter

  • 1 ½ cup gram flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Water as required
  • Salt to taste
  • ¼ tsp Turmeric powder

Steps

  1. Chop the onions, into thin slices.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and curry leaves. Cook till fragrant.
  3. Add asafoetida (hing) and mix well. Add the sliced onions and green chillies, saute till onion turns translucent.
  4. Add turmeric powder and salt, mix well and add little water, mix well.
  5. Add ginger garlic paste and mix well and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Mash the potatoes with your hands and add into the pan.
  7. Add water and cook for 3-4 minutes. Add sugar and mix well till it combines and melts.
  8. Allow to cool down.
  9. Meanwhile, prepare the batter. Add gram flour, baking soda and water to a bowl and whisk well forming a thick lump free batter.
  10. Add salt and turmeric powder, whisk it in and set aside till in use.
  11. Add chopped coriander to the cooled down potato mixture.
  12. Make round balls of the prepared mixture and dip them into the prepared batter.
  13. Deep fry the vadas till golden and crisp.
  14. Drain on an absorbent paper.
  15. Serve them hot inside the pav along with some bukha chutney sprinkled on top and green chillies on side.
